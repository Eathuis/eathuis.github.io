Meet Our Team

The PixelCC staff team is comprised of dedicated and hard working people. HA! Not really!


Josh Eathuis

Josh founded PixelCheeseCake in 2012 with the sole intention of making gaming videos with friends, having fun and generally relieving boredom. Since then he has kept the dream alive, with the help of his awesome pals! Josh does mainly Admin work, managing the various PixelCheeseCake accounts around the web, keeping all the information up to date, handling general uploads and maintaining the Staff Team as a whole. He is the self professed de facto Team Leader.

He likes nothing more than watching people take long walks on the beach, from a window in his bedroom to which he never leaves. Favourite games include: Minecraft, Terraria, Crash Bandicoot, Spiral Knights, Any Tower Defense Game, The Sonic Franchise, The Legend of Zelda Franchise and much much more. Most people call him boring!

Holly Hollz100

Holly is a needed driving force in the PixelCheeseCake team, joining in 2012 she was one of the first Team Members to be welcomed aboard the sinking life boat entitled "PixelCC" and eventually moving up the ranks to co-owner, owning the website, managing the staff, overseeing video quality and managing YouTube content, aswell as various "behind the scenes" Admin work. She has put alot of personal expense and hard work into PixelCheeseCake and it would not be alive today without her.

Her favourite colour is brown, she has every animal as a pet known to man, she dislikes Josh, is a Gandalf/Morgan Freeman and Kathy Bates Fanatic, preferred console of choice is the PlayStation (PS1 that is), additionally she likes Lucky Charms (the cereal)!

Joe MarcyC

As a founding member of PixelCheeseCake Joe has had a creative input from day 1, uploading the very first video to the YouTube channel titled "One off Games: ROBLOX - [Team Swap, Elimination and Base Defense] (Joe Plays)". Joe assisted Josh in writing the fist PixelCC contract on a scrunched up piece of A4 lined paper in 2013, which included soul ownership clauses and complete and utter control of "EVERYTHING". Joe doesn't really assist in the background work, instead focusing his attention on producing content.

Izzy SplodgeKat

Izzy (or SplodgeKat) joined the PixelCC crew in early 2014. Taking immediately to video editing she became PixelCheeseCake's very own in house editor, from the off! She has since moved up the ranks to Head Editor, overseeing the internal video production, which includes the audio/visual quality of videos.

She enjoys spending her time reading, sleeping and playing games with friends. Her everyday activities consist of cats, computer games, cheese and surfing the interwebs; she dislikes Spiders, blue cheese, loud sounds and Orcs. she may be a tiny bit obsessed with cats and from time to time revert to speaking in meows, but we just leave her to it.
